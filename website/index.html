About joni.bz

Our Mission

We believe domain registration should be as modern as the internet itself. By accepting cryptocurrency payments, we're making domain registration faster, more private, and accessible to everyone worldwide.

How We're Different

Traditional domain registrars require credit cards, personal information, and lengthy verification processes. We've streamlined everything with blockchain technology:

  • ✅ No credit card required

  • ✅ Complete privacy protection

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does registration take?

Once your payment is confirmed on the blockchain (typically 5-15 minutes), your domain is registered automatically within seconds.

What happens if my payment fails?

If payment doesn't arrive within 30 minutes, your order expires and no charge is made. You can create a new order anytime.

Do you store my cryptocurrency?

No. Payments go directly to our secure wallet addresses. We never hold funds in escrow.

Can I transfer my domain later?

Yes! All domains are standard registrations that can be transferred to any registrar using standard transfer procedures.

What if I need support?

Contact us at support@joni.bz and we'll respond within 24 hours.
