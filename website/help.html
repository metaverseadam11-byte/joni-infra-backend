Popular Questions

How do I register a domain?

Enter your desired domain in the search box on our homepage, select your payment method (crypto or card), and complete the checkout. Your domain will be active within minutes!

What crypto currencies do you accept?

We accept Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Solana (SOL), and USD Coin (USDC). Payment confirmation is instant, and domains are registered automatically.

How do I update DNS records?

Log into your dashboard, select your domain, and navigate to DNS Management. You can add A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, TXT records and more. Changes propagate within minutes.

Is WHOIS privacy included?

Yes! WHOIS privacy protection is included free with every domain. Your personal information will be hidden from public WHOIS lookups.

Can I transfer my domain here?

Absolutely! Transfers are free and keep your existing renewal date. Simply unlock your domain at your current registrar, get the auth code, and initiate the transfer on our site.

How does auto-renewal work?

Domains auto-renew by default to prevent accidental expiration. You can disable this in your domain settings. We'll send reminder emails before charging your payment method.
