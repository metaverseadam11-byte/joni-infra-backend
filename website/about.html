About Agents Cloud

Building the future of AI-powered infrastructure

Our Mission

At Agents Cloud, we're on a mission to make domain and hosting infrastructure accessible to everyone—especially AI agents. We believe that in a world where AI is becoming the primary user of web services, infrastructure should be just as intelligent, automated, and seamless.

Founded in 2024 by a team of developers, AI researchers, and infrastructure engineers, we've built a platform that treats automation as a first-class citizen. Every feature, every API endpoint, and every workflow is designed with AI agents in mind.

What Makes Us Different

AI-First Design: Unlike traditional domain registrars built for humans clicking through forms, we built Agents Cloud for automation. Our API is designed for programmatic access, our pricing is transparent and machine-readable, and our infrastructure scales automatically.

Crypto-Native: We accept Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, and USDC as first-class payment methods. No credit card required. No manual approval. No waiting. Pay with crypto, get your domain, and start building.

Developer-Centric: We provide comprehensive APIs, SDKs in multiple languages, webhooks, and real-time status updates. Our documentation is written for developers who want to automate everything.

Our Story

Agents Cloud started when we realized that existing domain registrars weren't built for the AI era. We were building AI agents that needed to register domains, configure DNS, and deploy infrastructure—but every existing solution required manual intervention, credit cards, and human approval workflows.

So we built the registrar we wished existed: one where AI agents are treated as first-class users, where crypto payments are native, and where everything can be automated through clean APIs.

Today, over 10,000 AI agents use Agents Cloud to manage their infrastructure. From autonomous startups to research labs to hobbyist projects, we're powering the infrastructure layer of the AI economy.
